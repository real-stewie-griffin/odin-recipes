Lentil soup (in Instant Pot)

Ingredients

  • Round lentils 200gr
  • Spicy Spanish chorizo 200gr
  • Big onions (1)
  • Medium-sized carrots (4)
  • Medium-sized potatoes (2)
  • Garlic cloves (4)
  • Stock cubes (2)
  • Cumin (2 tsp)
  • Paprika (2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Wash the lentils in a strainer, caring about removing any little stone if present
  2. Peel the onion and cut it in small pieces
  3. Peel the potatoes and the carrots and cut them in small pieces
  4. Peel the chorizo and cut it in small pieces
  5. Clean the garlic cloves and chop them in very small pieces
  6. Start the Instant Pot in 'Saut√©' mode and cook the chorizo during 10min
  7. Add the onions and the garlic and cook for another 20min
  8. Mix the 2 stock cubes, the paprika and the cumin and add all to the pot
  9. Add the carrots, the potatoes and the lentils and mix all together
  10. Add water until covering
  11. Cook for 15min high pressure. Use natural release
  12. Serve and enjoy
