Tortilla espa単ola

Description

Tortilla espa単ola is a Spanish omelette made of egg, potato, and onion cooked in olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in slices of 0.5cm aprox.
  2. Cut the onion in thin slices
  3. Prepare a pan with plenty of olive oil and heat it up
  4. Fry the potatoes together with the onion over medium heat
  5. Meanwhile, crack the eggs and mix them in a bowl
  6. Remove the fried potatoes and onions from the pan and salt them
  7. Mix them with the eggs and let it rest for at least 20min.
  8. Prepare a pan with some olive oil, heat it up and when is hot put the mix
  9. After 3-4 min. flip the tortilla over with help of a dish and cook for other 3-4 min.
  10. Serve and enjoy
