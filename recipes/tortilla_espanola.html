Tortilla espa単ola

Description

Tortilla espa単ola is a Spanish omelette made of egg, potato, and onion cooked in olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in slices of 0.5cm aprox.
  2. Cut the onions in thin slices
  3. Prepare a pan with plenty of olive oil and heat it up
  4. Fry the potatoes together with the onions over medium heat
  5. Meanwhile, crack the eggs and mix them in a bowl
  6. Remove the fried potatoes and onions from the pan and salt them
  7. Add them to the eggs and let the mix rest for at least 20min.
  8. Prepare a pan with some olive oil, heat it up and when it is hot, put in the mix
  9. After 4 min. flip the tortilla over with help of a dish or pan lid and cook for other 2 min.
  10. Serve and enjoy
