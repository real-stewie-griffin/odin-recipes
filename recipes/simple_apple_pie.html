Simple apple pie

Ingredients

  • Apples (6)
  • Eggs (4)
  • Milk 240ml
  • Sugar 225gr
  • Flour 180gr
  • Peach or apricot marmalade

Steps

  1. Peel all the apples and cut 4 of them in small pieces
  2. Mix in a bowl the 4 apples, the eggs, the sugar and the milk
  3. Grind everything until getting an homogeneous mass
  4. Add the flour to the mass. Integrate everything
  5. Butter a non-stick cake tin and put the mass in it
  6. Cut the other 2 apples in thin slices and place them on top of the mass
  7. Bake it at 180C during 60min
  8. Let the cake cool down
  9. Decorate the top with the marmalade using a brush
  10. Serve and enjoy
